Headings


A heading element implies all the font changes, paragraphs breaks before and after, and any white space necessary to render the heading. HTML defines six levels of headings.

  • < h1 >: is the highest (or most important) level.
  • < h2 >
  • < h3 >
  • < h4 >
  • < h5 >
  • < h6 >: is the least.
